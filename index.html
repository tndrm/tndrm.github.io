Eagle UP

It's an online 2D RPG inspired by D&D, functioning as a virtual gaming table. Users can act as Game Masters or players, with tools for communication, game management, and tasks like attacks, dice rolls, and creating ambiance.

I closely collaborated with the art team to implement environmental elements, including daytime and weather effects, as well as procedural landscape generation. I handled front-end and back-end development, including user registration and game settings. I also integrated ChatGPT and conducted refactoring and bug fixes.

Team size: 5
Development period: May - Sep 2023
